Welcome to my website!!!

About me:

Before we get into what this website is about, here’s a quick little intro about me. My name is Phuluso Leslie Nembilwi. I am a graduate from the University of Pretoria, where I majored in Applied Mathematics and Economics. My hobbies and interests include Mathematics, Music, Running, Art, Philosophy, Anime, and Writing.

Other random facts about me

  1. I love One Piece, it is my favourite show of all time, with Steins;Gate sitting in second place.
  2. My favourite philosopher is Aristotle, his virtue ethics from the Nicomachean is something I strive to live by.
  3. My favourite composer of all time is Beethoven. Not only does his music speak to me on so many levels, but his life story and how becoming deaf never stopped him from creating some of the greatest musical creations known to man inspire me.
  4. One of my biggest role models is Hannah Fry, a British mathematician and author. I love the way she explains complex mathematical concepts in a fun, engaging, and easy-to-understand way. She’s honestly the main inspiration behind my Mathematics section.

About this website

This website is basically a space for me to share the things I’m interested in. It's kind of like an open journal, where I write about topics I enjoy in a way that feels like a conversation rather than a lecture. So, the best way to read it is to imagine you’re just chatting with a friend who can’t stop talking about something they find fascinating. Hopefully, you’ll find something here that sparks your curiosity as well.
